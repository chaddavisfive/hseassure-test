Armed Robbery Awareness

Course Description

This course is designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills to respond appropriately during and after an armed robbery. The course emphasizes prevention, personal safety, and emotional resilience.

Formative Assessment

  • Scenario-based discussions and role-plays.

Summative Assessment

  • Multiple choice and situational response quiz at the end of the course. Minimum pass mark is 65%.

Course Outline

  • Understanding the nature of armed robberies
  • Recognizing high-risk environments
  • Appropriate responses during an armed robbery
  • Minimizing personal risk and ensuring group safety
  • Post-incident procedures and trauma response
  • Legal and reporting obligations

Course Information

Duration: 1 Day
Pre-Requisites: None
Target Groups:

  • Retail, hospitality, and security staff
  • Supervisors and front-line managers
  • Cash-handlers and transporters
