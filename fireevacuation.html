Fire Fighting & Evacuation

Course Description

This course enables learners to effectively deal with workplace fire emergencies by using the correct firefighting techniques and understanding evacuation protocols.

Formative Assessment

  • Practical demonstrations and fire drills.

Summative Assessment

  • Practical and written assessment. Learners must score 65% or more to be deemed competent.

Course Outline

  • Types and causes of fires
  • Fire prevention techniques
  • Classes of fires and types of extinguishers
  • Correct use of firefighting equipment
  • Evacuation planning and emergency exits
  • Roles of fire wardens and emergency response teams

Course Information

Duration: 1 Day
Pre-Requisites: None
Target Groups:

  • All employees
  • Emergency response teams
  • Safety officers and building supervisors
