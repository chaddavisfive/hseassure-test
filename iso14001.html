ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management

Course Description

This course covers the principles and requirements of the ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management System (EMS) standard. Learners gain insight into implementation, auditing, and compliance strategies.

Formative Assessment

  • Quizzes, case studies and group discussions.

Summative Assessment

  • Online written assessment with a pass mark of 65% or more.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to ISO 14001:2015
  • Environmental policy and planning
  • Legal and regulatory frameworks
  • Objectives, targets and programs
  • Monitoring and measuring environmental performance
  • EMS internal audits and continual improvement

Course Information

Duration: 2 Days
Pre-Requisites: None
Target Groups:

  • Environmental officers and coordinators
  • Compliance managers
  • Supervisors and operations teams
