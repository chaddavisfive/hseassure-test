ISO 45001:2015 Occupational Health & Safety

Course Description

This course provides a thorough understanding of the ISO 45001:2015 standard for Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems. Learners will understand how to implement, manage, and maintain systems that enhance workplace safety and regulatory compliance.

Formative Assessment

  • Workshops and group-based activities.
  • Standard interpretation exercises.

Summative Assessment

  • Written knowledge assessment. Learners must achieve 65% or more to pass.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to ISO 45001 and key terminology
  • Context of the organization and stakeholder analysis
  • Leadership and worker participation
  • Hazard identification and risk assessment processes
  • Operational controls and legal compliance
  • Performance evaluation and internal audits
  • Continual improvement and management review
  • Transitioning from OHSAS 18001 (if applicable)

Course Information

Duration: 2 Days
Pre-Requisites: Basic knowledge of occupational health and safety (recommended)
Target Groups:

  • Safety officers and HSE managers
  • Compliance and quality assurance teams
  • Supervisors and department heads
