ISO 9001:2015 – Quality Management Systems

Course Description

This course introduces learners to the principles and practices of ISO 9001:2015, the international standard for quality management systems (QMS). Participants will understand the requirements of the standard and how to implement or audit a QMS to improve consistency, performance, and customer satisfaction.

Formative Assessment

  • Interactive QMS exercises and real-world case studies
  • In-class group discussions and practice audits

Summative Assessment

  • Final written assessment – minimum 65% pass mark

Course Outline

  • Introduction to ISO 9001 and history of quality management standards
  • Understanding the structure of the High-Level Structure (Annex SL)
  • Key principles of quality management (Customer focus, Leadership, Engagement, etc.)
  • Clause-by-clause explanation of ISO 9001:2015 requirements
  • Risk-based thinking and continual improvement
  • Documentation and control of processes
  • Internal auditing techniques and compliance strategies
  • QMS implementation planning and common pitfalls

Course Information

Duration: 2 Days
Pre-Requisites: Basic business or management knowledge recommended
Target Groups:

  • Quality assurance personnel
  • Supervisors and team leaders
  • Internal auditors and QMS implementers
  • Managers seeking to improve product and service quality
